About Me

My name is Ibrahim Musa Sumayin. I’m passionate about graphic design and web development, and I enjoy creating designs that are both creative and meaningful. My journey started in 2023 when I helped a friend design a logo and flyer, and that experience inspired me to turn my interest into something bigger

I founded Zuko Designs, where I work on different design and web projects that bring ideas to life. I’ve learned through online courses and practice, earning certificates that helped me build a strong foundation in both fields

I’m always looking for new ways to improve my skills, learn from others, and create work that inspires people. For me, design isn’t just about visuals — it’s about telling stories and making ideas stand out
